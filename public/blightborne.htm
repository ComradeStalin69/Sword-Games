Blightborne

Support This Game

Blightborne is 2D Sidescrolling Platformer. Shoot, jump, dash and clear the dungeons in this adventure game.

Play as Rand the chosen one to defeat the evil lurking inside the dungeons and save his village. Find the long lost artifact which can bring life back to his village and their inhabitants.

Blighborne was in development for more than 9 months. I began working on it as a hobby project while attending university. Two years later I resumed working on it with a small team this time, but we parted ways and decided to release the game for free in the web.

**If you like the game, feel free to join my discord server and get beta access to all my future games. Become part of the team: https://discordapp.com/invite/RKDSWcb

Controls 

Left Arrow- Move Left

Right Arrow- Move Right

Up Arrow-Jump, Climb Ladder, Change Dash Direction

Down Arrow- Move Down Ladder, Change Dash Direction, Interact and peek down.

Z – Shoot

X – Dash

F – Open Ability Panel

A,S – Change Equipped Skill

C – Place / Take Blue Portal

V – Place / Take Red Portal

B – Return Back to Village

P – Open / Close Menu
